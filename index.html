My Work

Meander travel app

In a post-pandemic world, travelers are constantly seeking resources to ensure that their trips provide safe, engaging accommodations, but individuals have varying levels of comfortability with hitting the busiest spots. Meander allows travelers to locate local hotspots with real-time data, and provides a virtual hub to converse with locals to learn the ins-and-outs of a new place. In this project, I executed every part of the design process from initial ideation and interviews to wireframing and prototyping.

U.S. Department of Energy redesign

The U.S. Department of Energy website can be useful, but the information we seek is often hard to find. In this project, I created a website that is intuitive, attractive, and presents information clearly, all while portraying the powerful and credible feel of the government agency.

LAUNCH Skateboarding redesign

In this project, I worked with a team to identify a local nonprofit and work with the stakeholders to redesign their website.  We met with the owner, talked about what's working and what isn’t, and completed a full webpage redesign for mobile and desktop. We conducted several rounds of user testing, subsequently iterating our design and landing on a product that the owner loves.
